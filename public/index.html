Features

Built around how your body actually hydrates

Hydration Factor

Each drink is weighted by its real hydration contribution. Coffee counts less than water. Energy drinks count even less.

Caffeine Tracking

Monitor your daily caffeine intake alongside hydration. Get a warning when you're approaching your personal limit.

AI Insight

Get a personalized hydration tip generated from your actual data — drink patterns, goal progress, streak, and more.

Weekly & Monthly Stats

Calendar heat-map views show your goal achievement at a glance — colored cells for days you hit your target.

Alcohol Tracking

Log alcoholic beverages and track standard drinks per session. Supports 100+ drinks with ABV-based calculations.

Works Offline

Log drinks even without a connection. Logs are queued locally and synced to the cloud automatically when you're back online.

Drink Library

630+ drinks, fully searchable

Browse a curated library of beverages — each with its caffeine content and hydration factor pre-calculated. Add custom drinks, save favorites, and set up Quick Add shortcuts for your go-to drinks.
