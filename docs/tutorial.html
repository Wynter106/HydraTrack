User Guide

Getting started with HydraTrack

Follow these five steps to set up your account and start tracking your hydration. The whole process takes under two minutes.

1

Create your account

First-time users are taken directly to the Sign Up screen. Enter your email and a password to create your account.

  • New install opens Sign Up automatically
  • Already have an account? Tap Log in at the bottom
  • Your data syncs across devices via the cloud
Sign Up
Log In
2

Set up your profile

Enter your age, height, and weight. HydraTrack automatically calculates your personalized daily hydration goal and caffeine limit.

  • Hydration goal is based on your body weight (×33 ml/kg)
  • Caffeine limit adjusts based on your age
  • Supports oz/mL and lb/kg — switch any time in Settings
  • You can update these values later under Settings → Edit Profile
Profile Setup
3

Log your drinks

From the home screen, tap any drink in the Quick Add grid to instantly log it. For more options, tap More to browse the full drink library.

  • Quick Add shows your favorite drinks for one-tap logging
  • Tap Manage to customize which drinks appear
  • Drink library has 630+ beverages — searchable by name
  • Can't find your drink? Add a custom one with the + button
Home
4

Track your progress

Tap Log in the bottom navigation to see everything you've logged today, with the hydration factor applied to each drink.

  • Each entry shows actual hydration contributed, not just fluid volume
  • Coffee at factor 0.75 means 8 oz counts as 6 oz toward your goal
  • Tap the trash icon to remove any entry
  • Tap Weekly or Monthly for historical views
Today's Log
5

View stats and earn badges

The Goals tab shows your current badges and progress. The monthly calendar highlights every day you met your hydration goal.

  • Badges unlock as you build streaks and hit milestones
  • Calendar cells turn blue on days you hit your goal
  • Today's date is highlighted with a border
Goals
Monthly Stats
Bonus

Customize in Settings

Tap Settings in the bottom navigation to change your theme, set up daily drink reminders, or edit your profile.

Settings
    • Switch between Light, Dark, or System theme
    • Enable daily hydration reminders with a custom time
    • Enable medication reminders
    • Edit profile: update weight, height, or unit preference
