User Guide

Getting started with HydraTrack

Follow these steps to set up your account and start tracking your hydration. The whole process takes under two minutes.

1

Create your account

First-time users are taken directly to the Sign Up screen. Enter your email and a password to get started.

  • New installs open Sign Up automatically
  • Already have an account? Tap Log in at the bottom
  • Your data syncs across devices via the cloud
Sign Up
Log In
2

Set up your profile

Enter your age, height, and weight. HydraTrack automatically calculates your daily hydration goal and caffeine limit.

  • Hydration goal is based on body weight (×33 ml/kg)
  • Caffeine limit adjusts based on your age
  • Supports oz/mL and lb/kg — switch any time in Settings
  • Update these values later under Settings → Edit Profile
Profile Setup
3

Log your drinks

From the home screen, tap any drink in the Quick Add grid to instantly log it. Tap More to browse the full drink library.

  • Quick Add shows your saved favorites for one-tap logging
  • Tap Manage to customize which drinks appear
  • Drink library has 630+ beverages — search by name
  • The AI Insight card gives personalized hydration tips based on your data
Home
Drink Library
4

Create your own drink list

Build a personal drink list from the library. Star a drink to instantly save it as a favorite — no extra steps needed.

  • Tap the star on any drink to immediately add it to your favorites
  • Starred drinks appear in your Favorites tab and can be set as Quick Add shortcuts — all from one tap
  • Tap the pencil icon to further customize: change the icon, adjust the default volume, or toggle Quick Add on/off
  • Can't find your drink? Tap + Custom Drink to add your own with a custom name, size, and caffeine content
Edit Favorite
Custom Drink
5

Track your progress

Tap Log in the bottom navigation to see everything you've logged today, with the hydration factor applied to each drink.

  • Each entry shows actual hydration contributed, not just fluid volume
  • Coffee at factor 0.75 means 8 oz counts as 6 oz toward your goal
  • Tap Weekly or Monthly to see historical calendar views
  • Calendar cells turn blue on days you hit your goal
